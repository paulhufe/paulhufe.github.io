Paul Hufe

I am a Senior Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the University of Bristol. I am also affiliated with IZA, CESifo, IFS, and HCEO.

My research interests lie at the intersection of public, labor, and normative economics.

I hold a UKRI Future Leaders Fellowship (GBP 1.6m, 2024–2028) for the project “Unfair inequality in the labor market: a global study.”

Furthermore, I am currently a Co-I of the European Social Science Genetics Network — a doctoral network funded by the European Union’s Horizon Europe program.

I obtained my Ph.D. from LMU Munich and have been visiting Cornell University, Princeton University, and FAIR at NHH Bergen.
